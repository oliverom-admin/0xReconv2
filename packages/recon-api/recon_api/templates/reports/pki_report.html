Risk Exposure Metrics Explained

Assets at Risk

Count of unique assets (certificates and keys) that have at least one triggered policy finding.

% At Risk

Percentage of total assessed assets that have findings. Calculated as: Assets at Risk ÷ Total Assets × 100

% Exposure

Overall risk exposure derived from the Health Index (100 - Health %). Reflects the combined impact of finding severity and count. Lower is better.
